Uno Quizon

Rhino Ascension

About This

Rhino Ascension presents a striking encounter between the natural and the unknown. The solitary rhino stands as a symbol of strength and vulnerability, illuminated beneath an otherworldly presence that disrupts its environment. The hovering UFO introduces tension and mystery, transforming the scene into a meditation on intrusion, power, and survival. Through motion and atmosphere, the animation invites viewers to reflect on humanity’s impact, control, and the fragile boundary between dominance and coexistence.
