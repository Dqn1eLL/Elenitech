Kainah Salandanan

Starry Reflections

About This

In Search of Ariel unfolds as a gentle journey driven by curiosity and longing. The animation centers on the act of searching, using movement and environment to evoke wonder beneath the surface. Flowing motion both discovery and uncertainty, inviting viewers into a world where imagination and exploration merge. Rather than focusing on destination, the work celebrates the beauty of the pursuit itself.
