Hazel Tupas

Microsoft Animation

About This

The PowerPoint presentation focuses on 3D animation, using the Microsoft Surface Laptop as the main model. The concept is inspired by a product advertisement, where the laptop is presented through moving 3D visuals, timed text, and sound effects to make it more engaging. Different animations highlight the Surface Laptop’s design and features, while the use of sound and text helps guide the viewer’s attention and build interest. Even though it was made in PowerPoint, the presentation shows how 3D animation tools can be used creatively to produce a clean, ad-like result. Overall, the presentation aims to make the Surface Laptop look modern, dynamic, and appealing through motion and multimedia.
